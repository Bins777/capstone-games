📋 COMPLIANCE GLOSSARY — TAP TO EXPAND
These are the real frameworks governing your work — and that criminals exploit. Know them. They will come up during negotiations.
KYC — Know Your Customer▸
The legal obligation for financial institutions to verify the identity of clients, understand their source of funds, and assess money-laundering risk before onboarding. A KYC analyst's job is to ensure nobody hides behind a false identity or nominee structure. This is your job.
FATF — Financial Action Task Force▸
An intergovernmental body that sets global anti-money-laundering standards. Its 40 Recommendations are legally adopted by over 200 jurisdictions. Entirely legal and legitimate. When a stranger cites "FATF compliance," it may be genuine due diligence — or a smokescreen designed to sound legitimate.
FATF Rec. 24 — Beneficial Ownership▸
Requires that the real human owner behind any legal entity be identifiable and on record with authorities — not hidden behind nominee layers. Strengthened in 2022. If someone correctly cites this recommendation number by memory in casual conversation, pay attention. It may be a tell.
SPV — Special Purpose Vehicle▸
A legally registered subsidiary created for a narrow purpose — often to isolate financial risk. Legitimate in structured finance. Heavily abused in money laundering as a layering entity to obscure the origin of funds. The difference is transparency.
Nominee Director▸
A person who appears on company records as a director but acts only as a placeholder for the real beneficial owner. Legal in some jurisdictions. When used to hide the true controller of assets from regulators, it becomes a vehicle for financial crime — and the nominee becomes liable. This is the role they will offer you.
SAR — Suspicious Activity Report▸
A mandatory report filed by financial institutions when a transaction raises suspicion of money laundering. Filing is a legal obligation. Failing to file when you had reason to is a criminal offence in most jurisdictions. You have the power — and the duty — to file one.
